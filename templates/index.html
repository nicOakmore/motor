Generate a traceable budget from a construction memoria

Upload a memoria constructiva (text / Markdown) or try one of the samples. The engine maps scope items to partidas, prices them from the catalogue, applies the regulated build-up PEM → PEC → IVA (RD 1098/2001) and raises the Ibiza-specific regulatory flags.

1 · Try a sample memoria

2 · Or upload your own

Formats: .md, .txt. Max 256 KB. The parser recognises numbered items with a verb + measurement (e.g. «3. Enlucido de yeso: 80 m²»).

What you get

  • Budget (Markdown + JSON) — capítulos, partidas and summary cuadro.
  • Procurement plan (CSV) — consolidated materials with merma applied.
  • Regulatory flags — licencia previa, rústico, applicable IVA.
  • BC3 (FIEBDC-3) — exportable to Presto / Arquímedes / CYPE.
  • Full trace — every euro traces back to the rule that produced it.
